Mangroves provide a range of ecosystem services. These largely include the provision of timber, fuel wood, medicines,
natural dyes, honey, and marine food. At present, human communities within Indonesian mangrove environment often considered
mangrove ecosystem as an area of hope for the future. here are some example of the mangrove species and their uses :
-Aspleniumsp : Used as medicine
-Thespesiapopulnea : Used for boat building (ribs), fruit is used as toy, also as medicine
-Heritieralittoralis : Firewood, timber, fence, spots, used as medicine
